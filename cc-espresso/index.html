WHO WE ARE

C.C.'s is a local Duvall favorite with retro vibes and treats that the whole family will love. We love our small town community and contributing to everything Duvall has to offer.

Find us between the Rustic Rose gift shop and Country Collections antique store on Main Street across from the Duvall Library.
